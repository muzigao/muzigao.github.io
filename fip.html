FinanceInterviewPrep.com
Moonlyt,Inc. New York, NY

Description

FinanceInterviewPrep provides a good source for those looking to land a job in finance with access to finance professionals, and a platform for finance professionals helping the next generation of financiers.

For people who are looking for a job in finance and searching for knowledgeable people for mock interviews, career advice and resume critiques, FIP provides services including browsing finance professionals, hiring them for an online one-on-one video session via our interactive schedule module, secure payment, and posting board.

For financiers, who are willing to help the next generation and earn money during spare time, FIP is a good way for finding target audience. They could browse postings, bit one, have video session, build reputation by being reviewed, cash out, or view all transaction history.

In addition, users and coaches could interact with each other by forum or messaging system for useful templetes and quick Q&A.
