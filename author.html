AUTHOR'S SOCIAL NETWORK VISUALIZATION SYSTEM
Hunan University, Changsha, China

Description

This web-based system explored a visualized way to display authors' social relationship in academic fields.

Users could extract information of articles and authors. In addition, partnership among authors would be represented in a graphical diagram, indicating frequency of cooperation and importance of articles.

Participants: 1

Responsibility

• Explored algorithm of displaying social relationships among co-authors.

• Established a B/S system to present search results.
