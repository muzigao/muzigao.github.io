ABOUT ME

Hi, I am Muzi Gao, and welcome to my online home. As a student in Columbia University (New York, NY), I am now persuing my master degree in Computer Science. My interest is in Human Computer Interaction, User Experience and User Interface Design. Relevant courseworks include User Interface Design, Mobile HCI, Computer Graphics, Advanced Software Engineering, Introduction to Database, Analysis of Algorithms.

In the story of TOWER OF BABEL, God was scarred since people were like one person and they had one language. God confounded their speech and finally, people scattered, leaving off building the city, which was called Babel. Nowadays, inherent gulf between computer and human being exists. Reasons are various. Sometimes, users cannot understand "languages" and "expressions" used by applications. Or developers fail to figure out what users really need.

What I wish to do is thinking about new ideas, exploring users' needs and perspectives, trying different technologies and helping build better products. Link to my resume (pdf).
